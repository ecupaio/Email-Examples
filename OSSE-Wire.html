2014 Month of the Young Child

The Month of the Young Child™ is an annual celebration sponsored by the National Association for the Education of Young Children (NAEYC), the world's largest early childhood education association, with nearly 80,000 members and a network of over 300 local, state, and regional Affiliates. The purpose of the Month of the Young Child is to focus public attention on the needs of young children and their families and to recognize the early childhood programs and services that meet those needs

April Stands for Alcohol Awareness

The beginning of April commences alcohol awareness, a month-long healthy living campaign that promotes alcohol-free events that educate about the harms of underage drinking and binge drinking. Drinking too much alcohol can lead to health problems, including alcohol poisoning, hangovers, and an increased risk of heart disease. This April, Drug Free America Foundation encourages you to take this time to educate yourself and your loved ones about the dangers of alcohol abuse. For more information go to Healthfinder.gov 

HRA-NCA and Catholic University of America:  Certification Scholarship Being Offered

The Human Resource Association of the National Capital Area (HRA-NCA) and Catholic University of America (CUA) have a joint partnership that will provide a scholarship to an active member of HRA-NCA pursuing a Professional in Human Resources (PHR) or Senior Professional in Human Resources (SPHR) certification through the Human Resource Certification Institute (HRCI).  CUA is the sole official university provider of the SHRM Learning System in Washington, DC.  The scholarship covers the tuition and SHRM learning materials associated with attending one of the SHRM Learning System courses that CUA will offer in the Fall of 2014. View more information on CUA's course schedule.  (Course schedule information is also provided on the enclosed application materials.)  The HRA-NCA College Relations and Scholarship Committee(s) administer the scholarship program.

Eligibility: Any HRA-NCA member in good standing who meets the requirements of certification eligibility as determined by HRCI is eligible to apply for this scholarship.  HRA-NCA Board members and the College Relations and Scholarship Committee(s) members are not eligible.

Applicants who are awarded a scholarship and become PHR-certified may reapply for this scholarship toward the SPHR certification provided they have waited one year after receipt of award.  Previously eligible applicants are allowed to reapply each year, provided they continue to meet the eligibility requirements.

Selection: The recipient will be selected on the basis of work experience and commitment to a career in HR. Recipient must enroll in the certification prep course at CUA immediately following notification of the award. Scholarship recipient(s) must complete the application forms for the certification prep course and the PHR/SPHR exam and forward copies of them to the College Relations and Scholarship Committee(s) of HRA-NCA.

Deadline: All application materials must be submitted to HRA-NCA at the address indicated by Friday, September 5, 2014.

Application Checklist: The application is complete and valid upon receipt of the application package by the deadline. Each application package must include the following:

  1. Signed Application (download here);

  2. Resume;

  3. Essay;

  4. Two (2) Letters of Professional Recommendation.

Send all documents via email to info@hra-nca.org.  Hard copies may be mailed to HRA-NCA Scholarship Committee: P.O. Box 2153, Springfield, VA, 22152. Although email is preferred and hard copies are not necessary.
